SMALL SERVICE ACTIONS

A guest attending a Disney Institute class at Walt Disney World sat down on an outdoor bench during a break. When he got up, he noticed his pants were soaked. The sprinklers had just been turned off and the guest did not notice the bench was wet.

Just as he was wondering what to do, a Disney cast member passed by and asked him how his day was going. He glanced down at his dripping pants and said, "Fine, but don't sit on that bench."

Instantly the cast member phoned to have a hair dryer delivered and the benches dried. The cast member then led the guest to a restroom so he could blow-dry his pants before returning to class.

By taking the little extra step, providing that little "wow" our cast member created a positive memory for our guest.
