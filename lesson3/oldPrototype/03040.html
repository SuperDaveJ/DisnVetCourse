Disney's Approach to Quality Service

Many organizations believe that the effective delivery of their service is a competitive advantage. Consumers certainly think so. Their quality service expectations have increased dramatically over the years.

Price, quality, and unique product features are expected. But expected features are not enough for repeat business. People don't just buy things. They buy, and expect, high levels of performance and guest service: even in healthcare.

This course will show you how the Disney Quality Service philosophy has made Walt Disney Park and Resorts a phenomenal success year in and year out. We will focus on the ways Walt Disney Parks and Resorts meets, and then exceeds, guests' needs.

Meeting needs is not enough. Our Disney Parks and Resorts strategy is to exceed our guests' already very high expectations. The tactic we use is to Pay Attention to every Detail of the Delivery.
