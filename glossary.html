Cast Member - any employee who works for Disney Parks and Resorts

Common Purpose - an inspirational and motivational tool companies use to set short-term and long term service goals; the big picture of the organization's mission that cast members use to make decisions on a daily basis

Courtesy - treating every guest like a VIP, Very Individual Person, and respecting guest diversity; a Disney Quality Standard

Demographics - facts about guests such as age and home city and state used in planning and budgeting

Efficiency - providing the smoothest possible operation of Disney Parks and Resorts; a Disney Quality Standard

Experience Map - a graphic method for analyzing the Disney guest experience at any touchpoint resulting in actionable and achievable behaviors that will meet and also exceed expectations

Guest - any visitor to a Disney Park and Resort

Guestology - demographic and psychographic information about guests that gives Disney cast members clarity about who guests are and what they expect

Guestology Compass - a graphic compass model of guest psychographic information - needs, wants, stereotypes and emotions - used to understand the guest experience so that needs can be met and/or service improved

Little Wows - small details that exceed guest expectations in a way that is a surprise and a delight

Psychographics - guest expectations, commonly thought of as needs, wants, stereotypes and emotions

Quality - a high degree of excellence

Quality Service - meeting a person's needs with a high degree of excellence

Quality Standards - tools Disney cast members use to make the right decision regardless of the circumstance; operational priorities, or criteria that ensure consistent delivery of guest service

Safety - providing for the physical welfare of guests and maintaining their peace of mind; a Disney Quality Standard

Service - meeting a person's needs

Show - delivering a seamless performance while never allowing reality to interfere with fantasy; a Disney Quality Standard

Small Service Actions - small acts that exceed guest expectations

Touchpoint - any category of interaction between the guest and a Disney Park and Resort; used in experience mapping to identify a guest experience and analyze it thoroughly until actionable and achievable behaviors that will improve that experience can be defined

VA - Veterans Administration

Veteran Centered Care - putting the Veteran first at the center of every action

VIP - very individual person; at Disney Parks and Resorts, every guest is a VIP whose diversity needs to be respected
