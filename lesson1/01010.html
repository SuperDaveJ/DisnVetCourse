Welcome to the Course on Veteran Centered Care

During this course, you will:

  • Explore Disney's proven model for delivering world-class guest service and how it relates to VISN 18
  • Discover and utilize tools for adapting new ideas and best practices to VISN 18
  • Analyze your existing processes and suggest improvements

  • A sense of pride in "serving America's heroes," and
  • A renewed sense of purpose
