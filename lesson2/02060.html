Narrator

You may not walk out of your office into the shadow of a castle with real live princesses on site, but there are similarities between our organization and yours. The Walt Disney Company is in the entertainment industry, but the business issues, such as budget constraints, staffing, labor management, right-sizing, and cost-shifted medical insurance, are real.

In our own way, we too are in the business of caring for people.

Unfortunately, our service strategies and tactics, which have been woven into the fabric of Disney culture, do not change people's personalities. We do have management problems such as miscast cast members, service failures and internal politics. And sometimes, we just get it wrong.

But we have found that the strategies and tactics we will explore today will dramatically increase the likelihood of turning random acts of kindness into business as usual at VISN 18.
