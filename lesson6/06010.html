What Are Quality Standards?

Quality Standards provide employees with the tools needed to make the right decisions. They are the operational priorities, or criteria, that ensure consistent delivery of guest service.

Quality Standards:

  • Set organizational and cast parameters for decision-making
  • Prioritize the details of service delivery
  • Allow consistent measurement of service delivery

Narrator

As we discovered in the last module, Todd and Spencer are expected to balance the needs of their task and the requirement to deliver on the common purpose.

Similar to VISN 18, Disney cast members may be faced with an issue that requires an immediate decision, and they may be in an area where supervisors or other cast members are not available. It is important tools are available so the right decision can be made quickly. We call these Quality Standards.

Quality Standards help cast members balance seemingly competing needs, and are the operating priorities that help deliver consistent service.
