Disney's Quality Standards

Narrator

The Disney Quality Standards are

  • Courtesy
  • Efficiency
  • Safety
  • Show

These four words help clarify expectations for our cast members.

  • Courtesy – Treating every guest like a VIP, a very individual person, and respecting the diversity of our guests
  • Efficiency - Providing the smoothest possible operation of our parks and resorts
  • Safety – Providing for the physical welfare of our guests and maintaining their peace of mind
  • Show – Delivering a seamless performance. Never allowing reality to interfere with fantasy
